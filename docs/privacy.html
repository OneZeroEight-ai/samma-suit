Privacy Policy

Effective: February 15, 2026

This policy describes how OneZeroEight.ai ("we", "us") collects and uses data when you use Sammā Suit at sammasuit.com.

What We Collect

  • Email address — provided at sign-up, used for account identification and transactional emails (welcome, billing alerts, password resets).
  • Authentication data — managed by Clerk. We store a Clerk user ID linked to your account. Clerk handles passwords, OAuth tokens, and session cookies.
  • API usage logs — every request through the Sammā Suit gateway is logged in the audit trail (SILA layer): timestamp, action, token counts, cost. This is a core security feature.
  • Billing information — managed by Stripe. We store your Stripe customer ID. Stripe handles card numbers, payment details, and invoices. We never see or store your card number.

BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)

If you provide your own LLM API key (e.g., Anthropic), it is encrypted using Fernet symmetric encryption and stored in our database. Your key is decrypted only at the moment of an API call and is never logged, shared, or transmitted to any party other than the LLM provider you chose.

Cookies

We use Clerk session cookies to keep you signed in. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics cookies. There is no cross-site tracking.

Third-Party Services

  • Clerk — authentication and session management. Clerk Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — subscription billing and payment processing. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic — LLM API calls are made using your own API key (BYOK model). We do not send your data to Anthropic on our behalf.

Data Location

Our backend runs on Railway (US-based infrastructure). Database backups are stored in the same region. We are planning a migration to an Iceland-based VPS for improved data sovereignty.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data. Ever.
  • We do not serve ads.
  • We do not share your data with third parties beyond the services listed above.
  • We do not train AI models on your data or conversations.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing info@sammasuit.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days. This includes your customer record, audit logs, agent configurations, and any stored API keys.

Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered users.

Questions? Contact info@sammasuit.com
